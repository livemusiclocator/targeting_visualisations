Music Venue Analysis Dashboard

  • Interactive LGA Venues Chart

    This interactive bar chart displays the number of music venues for each Local Government Area (LGA). It is designed for easy exploration and comparison.

    • Y-Axis: Local Government Area (LGA) name.
    • X-Axis: The total number of unique music venues recorded in that LGA.
    • Interaction: Use the filter controls at the top to display only the LGAs that fall within a specified range of venue counts (Min to Max). Click "Apply Filter" to update the chart.
  • Interactive Tourism Venues Chart

    This chart is similar to the one above but aggregates the data by Tourism Region instead of LGA, providing a broader geographical perspective.

    • Y-Axis: Tourism Region name.
    • X-Axis: The total number of unique music venues recorded in that Tourism Region.
    • Interaction: Use the filter controls to show only the regions with a venue count within your specified range. Click "Apply Filter" to see the results.
  • LGA Revenue vs. Venues Bubble Chart

    This interactive bubble chart explores the relationship between an LGA's financial resources and its number of music venues. The size and color of the bubbles provide additional layers of information.

    • Y-Axis: Council Revenue in millions of Australian dollars (AUD).
    • X-Axis: The total number of unique music venues in the LGA.
    • Bubble Size: Represents the total combined capacity of all venues in the LGA. Larger bubbles indicate a higher total capacity.
    • Bubble Color: Represents the average venue capacity in that LGA, with a new "Plasma" color scale.
    • Interaction: Hover your mouse over any bubble to see a detailed tooltip with specific data for that LGA. Use the dropdown menu to select and highlight a specific LGA with a bright magenta circle.
  • LGA Revenue vs. Population Bubble Chart

    This chart examines the relationship between an LGA's council revenue and its population size, with bubble size indicating the number of venues.

    • Y-Axis: Council revenue in millions of Australian dollars (AUD).
    • X-Axis: Population of the LGA.
    • Bubble Size: Represents the number of music venues in the LGA. Larger bubbles mean more venues.
    • Interaction: This is a static chart for direct comparison.
